Your Trello Power-Up is running successfully!

Features

  • Card Badges: Displays member count and checklist completion on card fronts
  • Card Buttons: Adds custom action buttons in card detail view
  • Board Buttons: Adds settings button in board header

Setup Instructions

  1. Deploy this Power-Up to Netlify or your hosting platform
  2. Go to Trello Power-Ups Admin
  3. Click "Create New Power-Up"
  4. Enter your Power-Up details
  5. Set the Iframe Connector URL to: https://your-site.netlify.app/connector.js
  6. Enable the Power-Up on your board

Customize

Edit connector.js to customize the badges, buttons, and functionality.
